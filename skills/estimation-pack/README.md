# estimation-pack

## Overview

Internal operating pack for CreateFuture pre-sales leads, delivery leads, architects, product leads, and commercial leads. It supports opportunity qualification, estimate shaping, delivery approach recommendations, and proposal-ready outputs.

## When to use it

- You need to decide whether an opportunity is estimate-ready.
- You need a red-flag check before committing to a delivery shape or proposal.
- You need targeted client questions, assumptions, risks, or a decision log.
- You need to calibrate AI-native delivery estimates against tooling maturity.
- You need to shape an Activation, architectural review, AI showcase, or delivery phase.

## Example prompts

- "Use `estimation-pack` to assess whether this brief is ready to estimate."
- "Use `estimation-pack` to produce a question pack and delivery-shape recommendation."
- "Use `estimation-pack` to calibrate this opportunity and flag the confidence level."
